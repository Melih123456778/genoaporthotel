About Genoa Port Hotel

A calm and elegant hotel stay in one of Istanbul’s most historic districts.

Genoa Port Hotel is a modern and refined hotel located in Karaköy, one of Istanbul’s most vibrant and culturally rich districts. Designed for travelers who value comfort, calm and authenticity, the hotel offers thoughtfully designed rooms, elegant interiors and a peaceful atmosphere within the dynamic rhythm of the city. With its central location, Genoa Port Hotel provides effortless access to Galata, the historic peninsula, cultural landmarks, restaurants and business hubs, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure stays in Istanbul.

Location

Genoa Port Hotel is located in the heart of Karaköy, one of Istanbul’s most vibrant and cultural districts. Our central location offers effortless access to the city’s iconic landmarks, shopping areas, and transportation hubs. From historic attractions like Galata Tower to modern destinations such as Galataport, guests can explore the city comfortably on foot or within minutes by tram, ferry, or taxi.
