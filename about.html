About Genoa Port Hotel

Timeless Comfort in Karaköy

Genoa Port Hotel is a boutique hotel located in the heart of Karaköy, one of Istanbul’s most vibrant and historic districts.

Our philosophy is simple: warm hospitality, refined interiors and a calm retreat within the rhythm of the city.

Whether you are visiting for leisure or business, we aim to offer a welcoming and memorable stay.
